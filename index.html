Scavenger Hunt

📜 How to Play

  • Setup: Player 1 types something to find outside. It must be an outdoor item (like a leaf, rock, or stick) or the game will make you try again!
  • The Hunt: Player 2 has 10 minutes to track it down.
  • Proof: Snap and upload a picture of the item.
  • Scoring:
    • If it's correct: +10 points!
    • If it's wrong or time runs out: 0 points.
  • Switching: Players swap roles every round.
  • Winning: First person to reach 100 points wins!
